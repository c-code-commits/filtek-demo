FilterCart Skid FC-2

Portable filtration powerhouse for versatile hydraulic applications

Product Overview

The FilterCart Skid FC-2 combines mobility with industrial-grade filtration performance. This rugged, portable system is designed for professionals who need flexible, high-capacity filtration across multiple locations and applications.

Built on a heavy-duty steel frame with industrial casters, the FC-2 easily navigates workshop environments. Its versatile design accommodates various applications including fluid transfer, reservoir flushing, kidney loop filtration, and new oil conditioning.
