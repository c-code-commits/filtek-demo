DFU-60 Hydraulic Oil Filtration Unit

High-performance filtration system engineered for demanding hydraulic applications

Product Overview

The DFU-60 Hydraulic Oil Filtration Unit represents the pinnacle of modern filtration technology. Designed for continuous operation in demanding industrial environments, this unit delivers exceptional contamination removal while maintaining optimal flow rates.

Featuring advanced multi-stage filtration technology, the DFU-60 effectively removes particulates, water, and contaminants to maintain ISO 4406 cleanliness standards. Its robust construction and efficient design ensure reliable performance with minimal maintenance requirements.
