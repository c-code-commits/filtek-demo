Product Overview

The HDI-2 Hilsorb Dryer Cartridge represents a breakthrough in hydraulic oil moisture removal technology. Utilizing our proprietary Hilsorb desiccant material, this cartridge delivers exceptional water absorption capacity while maintaining optimal oil flow characteristics.

Engineered for compatibility with Filtrade filtration units, the HDI-2 effectively removes both free and dissolved water, protecting critical hydraulic components from moisture-related degradation. The integrated saturation indicator provides visual confirmation of cartridge condition.

Why Moisture Removal Matters

Component Corrosion

Water contamination accelerates oxidation and corrosion of critical metal components.

Reduced Lubricity

Moisture degrades oil film strength, increasing wear on pumps, valves, and cylinders.

Additive Depletion

Water reacts with oil additives, compromising fluid performance characteristics.

Bacterial Growth

Moisture promotes microbial contamination, creating sludge and system fouling.
