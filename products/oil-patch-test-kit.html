Oil Patch Test Kit

Rapid field testing solution for hydraulic oil contamination analysis

Product Overview

The Filtrade Oil Patch Test Kit is an essential tool for rapid, on-site assessment of hydraulic oil contamination levels. This comprehensive testing kit enables maintenance professionals to quickly evaluate fluid cleanliness without the need for laboratory analysis.

Using proven patch test methodology, this kit provides visual representation of particulate contamination, allowing for immediate assessment and informed maintenance decisions. Perfect for preventive maintenance programs and quality control verification.
