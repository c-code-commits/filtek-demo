Moisture Sensor

Real-time moisture monitoring for proactive hydraulic system protection

Product Overview

The Filtrade Moisture Sensor provides continuous, real-time monitoring of water content in hydraulic oils. This advanced sensing system enables proactive maintenance management by detecting moisture contamination before it causes system damage or component failure.

Featuring precision capacitance-based sensing technology, the moisture sensor delivers accurate measurements across a wide range of oil types and operating conditions. The integrated digital display and programmable alarm outputs make it an essential tool for condition monitoring programs.

Early detection of moisture ingress enables corrective action before component degradation occurs.

Extend Oil Life

Maintaining proper moisture levels preserves oil quality and extends fluid service intervals.
